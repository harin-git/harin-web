I'm interested in decoding various aspects of cultural patterns. From the aesthetic choices we make as individuals (like selecting the perfect soundtrack for a Sunday brunch with friends), to how culture evolves and diffuses across cities and countries (how did k-pop became a global phenomenon?). To achieve this, I gather massive datasets of music all around the world from archival recordings to online streaming data. I combine them with behavioural experiment paradigms by running cross-cultural experiments online using PsyNet and make field trips to collect data in the wild (such as in the Bolivian Amazon).
